Delicious Chicken Burger Recipe

Description:

A juicy and flavorful chicken burger recipe that's perfect for any meal!

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, and onion slices
  • Your favorite condiments (mayo, ketchup, mustard)

Steps

  1. Preheat your grill or skillet to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Cook the chicken for 6-7 minutes on each side or until fully cooked.
  4. Toast the burger buns on the grill for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Assemble the burgers by placing the cooked chicken on the bottom bun, followed by lettuce, tomato, onion, and your favorite condiments.
  6. Top with the other half of the bun and serve immediately.
